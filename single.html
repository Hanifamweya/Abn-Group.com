ABN GROUP

ABN GROUP ( Z ) LTD  COMPANY PROFILE & GENERAL,SUP ABN GROUP is a Private Limited Company registered in Zambia, established and operating as independent Investment Advisory Management Consultancy Firm ,whose focus is on Infrastructure Business Development , outsourcing Finances from various Export Financing Agencies, Transactional Advisors in various Investments portfolios. ABN GROUP is a Group of Companies in Zambia SADC/COMESA region. The Group consists of ABN GROUP LTD, ABN INDUSTRIES LTD ,ABN AIRLINES LTD, ABN TRANSPORT AND LOGISTICS, ABN ASSET MANAGEMENT LTD ,ABN ENERGY LTD, ABN FINANCE LTD , ABN TECHNOLOGY LTD, EUROSTOCKS LTD, MEDRIC INVESTMENTS LTD, BDN MINING LTD, AFRICA BUSINESS NETWORKS SARL, ABN AIRLINES SARL, ABN TZ LTD, ABN GROUP PTY, ABN ANGOLA LTDA, ABN MINING ENGINEERING LTD. ABN PHARMACEUTICALS LTD and ABN MINERALS LTD

ABN POLICY ABN policy caters for all the facets diverse Business promotion and service range. Our strategy is to create an effective movement of information within the region on opportunities that may exist in affiliate countries and match the same with investors and business clients looking for particular areas to seed their resources, as well as penetrate new markets.
